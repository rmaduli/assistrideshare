Get to Know Us!

We ignite opportunity by Creation

The world is made with unique and divine people of different shapes and sizes. Our mission is to improve the lives of our neighbors who need a little extra assistance! Good things happen when people can move, whether it is to the town next door or the city 6 hours away. Our goal was to create a platform that specializes in helping those who need it.

Vision

Our vision is to improve the lives of the people around the world with transportation suitable for everyone! When you sign up to ride for our services, be sure to mention what makes you special, happy, and most importantly, the needs you want us to satisfy! We offer Vans that are wheelchair accessible, student and senior discounts, and more!


No matter what, we will strive to ASSIST you in any kind of transportation services! Come join our family today and we will email you back a confirmation on your profile as well as any other specific requests we may need from you (ie. a picture of your driver's license when you sign up to Drive).
