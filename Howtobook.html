How Can We Assist You?

Step One

Creating an account is easy! Click below to sign up. Then all you need is an email address and a phone number. This is key in order to browse for a ride in the nearby area as a driver will be located and directed to you and have your phone number in case of possible questions.

Sign up to Ride


Step Two

Once you are signed up, we will look at your profile to see what specific needs to give you. Based on your information, we can determine what kind of car you need (ex. wheelchair accessible bus). To get you to your next destination, you can submit a request through our website! Enter the address of your choice in the search bar “Where to?” Then click “confirm pick up” and then you should be on your way shortly.


Step Three

When meeting the driver for pickup, make sure you are with your correct driver. Ask your driver for his name and who he is picking up. Double check to make sure that you are entering the right vehicle you were assigned to by checking the license plate.


Step Four

Once in the vehicle, sit back and enjoy the ride. Connect with others when you are in our safety buses! Watch your destination in real time. Please give feedback and rate the driver/ride as you wish. We want to hear from you! See you next time!
